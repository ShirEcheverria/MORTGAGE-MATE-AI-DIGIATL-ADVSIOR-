## 📌 About The Project
Taking a mortgage is one of the biggest financial decisions a family makes. However, the process is heavily bureaucratic, and bank offers are filled with complex financial jargon. 
Mortgage Mate solves this by acting as a digital advisor that translates, compares, and calculates mortgage tracks in a transparent and neutral way.

## ✨ Key Features (MVP)
1. **🤖 AI Mortgage Translator:** Users can upload their bank mortgage offers (via PDF/Image). The system uses OCR and LLM to ingest the data and translate complex financial terms into simple, everyday language.
   
2. **📊 Smart Comparison Dashboard:** A clean, intuitive dashboard that allows users to compare different mortgage tracks side-by-side without the bias of bank recommendations.

3. **🧮 Refinance Calculator:** Users can upload an active mortgage statement to calculate potential refinancing options and see long-term cost curves and savings over a 30-year timeline.

## 🚀 Future Roadmap & Vision
* **Real-Time Data Sync:** Direct connection to official information sources (Bank of Israel, inflation index) for automatic interest rate updates.
* **Advanced Financial Modeling:** Full support for complex Israeli mortgage mixes, early repayment considerations, and multi-track comparisons.
* **Goal Tracking & Dedicated Investment Portfolio:** The system will allow defining the missing down payment as a "Financial Goal" and offer a synced investment portfolio. It will track progress, alert on high-risk repayment metrics, and trigger a "Goal Reached!" notification when the gap is closed—enabling a safe transition to underwriting.
* **Strict "Math Isolation":** Deepening capabilities while maintaining the AI strictly as an accessibility tool, without making financial decisions for the user.

## 🛠️ Architecture & Tech Stack
The MVP demonstrates a "Strict Math Isolation" architecture where the AI layer is separated from the calculation engine.
* **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Component-based UI)
* **Backend Concepts:** `server.js`, `db.js`, `auth.js`, `config.js`
* **Integrations:** AI OCR & LLM processing, Routing & State Management.
